Stop Gun Violence in NYC

This is real. This is personal. It's time to change.

The Reality

Gun violence is the leading cause of premature death in the U.S., costing the country $557 billion yearly.

In New York alone, it's a $11.4 billion crisis. In 2022, 44,231 people died and 38,498 were injured by firearms nationally.

Black Americans are 10x more likely to be killed and 18x more likely to be injured by guns than white Americans.

Take Action

  • Speak up. Report illegal guns anonymously.
  • Organize or attend community meetings.
  • Volunteer with anti-violence groups in your area.
  • Spread the word on social media using #StopGunViolenceNYC.

Resources & Hotlines

If you find a gun or have one to dispose of safely:

  • 📞 NYPD Gun Tip Line: 1-866-GUN-STOP
  • 📞 NYC Crisis Line: 1-888-NYC-WELL
  • 🔗 Submit Anonymous Tip Online

If you want to help, join groups like Everytown for Gun Safety or Giffords.
